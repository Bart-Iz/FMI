Zadanie 1. Obliczyć podane całki nieoznaczone:

  • a) \( \int e^{x-3} \, dx = \operatorname{e}^{x-3} \)
  • n) \( \int \frac{5 - x}{\sin^2(x)} \, dx = (x-5)\operatorname{ctg}(x) - \operatorname{ln}(\operatorname{ctg}(x)) \)
  • b) \( \int \sqrt[3]{2x + 3} \, dx = \frac{3}{8}(2x + 3)^{4/3}\)
  • o) \( \int 2xe^{x^2} (1 + x^2) \, dx = x^2\operatorname{e}^{x^2} \)
  • c) \( \int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{4 - 9x^2}} = \frac{1}{3}\operatorname{arcsin}(\frac{3}{2}x) \)
  • p) \( \int \sin^5(x) \cos(x) \, dx = \frac{\operatorname{sin}^6(x)}{6}\)
  • d) \( \int \frac{2}{\cos^2(2x)} \, dx = \operatorname{tg}(2x)\)
  • q) \( \int \frac{e^x}{2 + e^x} \, dx = \operatorname{ln}(2+\operatorname{e}^x) \)
  • e) \( \int \frac{1 + \ln(x)}{x} \, dx = \operatorname{ln}(x)*(1+\frac{\operatorname{ln}(x)}{2})\)
  • r) \( \int \frac{e^{4x} - e^{-x}}{2} \, dx = \frac{\operatorname{e}^{-x}}{8}(\operatorname{e}^{5x}+4) \)
  • f) \( \int \cos^2(x) \sin(x) \, dx = -\frac{\operatorname{cos}^3(x)}{3} \)
  • s) \( \int x^{13} \ln(x) \, dx = \frac{x^{14}}{14}(\operatorname{ln}(x)-\frac{1}{14}) \)
  • g) \( \int a^{5x} \ln(a) \, dx = \frac{1}{5}a^{5x}\)
  • t) \( \int x^2 \sin(3x) \, dx = \frac{1}{27}\operatorname{cos}(3x)(2-9x^2) + \frac{2x}{9}\operatorname{sin}(3x) \)
  • h) \( \int (7x - 1) \cos(x) \, dx = \operatorname{sin}(x)(7x-1)+7\operatorname{cos}(x) \)
  • u) \( \int [3(x - 1)^2 - 4(x - 1)] \cos(x - 1) \, dx = \operatorname{sin}(x-1)(3x^2-10x+1) + \operatorname{cos}(x-1)(6x-2) \)
  • i) \( \int \ln(x) \, dx = x(\operatorname{ln}(x)-1) \)
  • v) \( \int \frac{arctg^3(2x)}{4x^2 + 1} \, dx = \frac{\operatorname{arctg}^4(2x)}{8} \)
  • j) \( \int (2 + x)e^{x^2} \, dx = \operatorname{e}^{x}(1+x) \)
  • w) \( \int \sin(x) \cdot e^{\cos(x)} \, dx = -e^{\operatorname{cos}(x)} \)
  • k) \( \int xe^{-x} \, dx = -\operatorname{e}^{-x}(x+1) \)
  • x) \( \int \operatorname{tg}(x) \, dx = -\operatorname{ln}(\operatorname{cos}(x)) \)
  • l) \( \int \sqrt{4x} \ln(x) \, dx = \frac{4}{9}x^{3/2}(3\operatorname{ln}(x)-2) \)
  • y) \( \int \frac{\operatorname{tg}(x)}{\cos^2(x)} \, dx = \frac{\operatorname{tg}^2(x)}{2} \)
  • m) \( \int \frac{\ln(x)}{x^3} \, dx = -\frac{2\operatorname{ln}(x)+1}{2x^2} \)
  • z) \( \int \frac{\cos(x)}{\sqrt{\sin(x)} + 5} \, dx = 2\sqrt{\operatorname{sin}(x)+5} \)
